Fettucine Alfredo

Description

A vegan recipe for fettucine alfredo by America's Test Kitchen. Serves 4

Ingredients

  • 2.5 cups unsweetened almond milk
  • .33 cups refined coconut oil
  • 3 Tbsp white miso
  • Salt and Pepper to taste
  • .75 cups raw cashews, blended
  • 1 lb pasta, bowtiws or fettucine work best
  • Pinch ground nutmeg
  • parsley to taste

Steps

  1. Combine almond milk, oil, miso and 1 tsp salt in a large saucepan and bring to simmer of medium heat, whisking to dissolve miso. Stir in blended cashews, reduce heat to medium low and cook partially covered for 10-20 minutes.
  2. Meanwhile, cook pasta to package direcions. Reserve .5 cups pasta water before draining pasta.
  3. Transfer sauce to empty pasta pot. Whisk in nutmeg and bring to gentle simmer over medium-low heat. Add drained pasta and cook while stirring until sauce is thickened. Adjust consistency with pasta water as needed.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Sprinke with parsley and vegan parmesean to tatse.
