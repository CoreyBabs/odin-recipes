Fried Seitan Nuggets

Description

Vegan fried Seitan using a modified recipe from Gaz Oakley. Serves 4.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Seitan
  • 2 cups/500 ml Soy milk
  • Juice from 1 lemon
  • .5 cups or 60g AP flour
  • .5 cups or 60g cornstarch
  • 2 tsp black pepper
  • 2 tsp ground ginger
  • 2 tsp cayenne pepper (optional)
  • 2 tsp dried oregano
  • 2 tsp smoked paprika
  • 3 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp dried thyme
  • 2 tsp celery salt (optional)
  • 2 tbs caster or cane sugar
  • lots of oil for frying

Steps

  1. Whisk together soy milk and lemon juice. Set aside for 5 minutes.
  2. Combine all dry coating ingredients.
  3. Dip seitan into flour mix, then into the buttermilk, then back into the flour mix. Make sure everything is well coated.
  4. Add oil to wok or large saucepan and heat over medium heat. If a crumb of batter bubbles when dropped into oil, it is ready to fry.
  5. Add seitan to oil in batches. Cook for 5-6 minutes per side, or until golden brown and crispy on all sides.
  6. Place onto a paper towel lined plate and immediately season with salt.
