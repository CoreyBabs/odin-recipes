Vegan Lomo Saltado

Description

A veganized lomo saltado recipe from little spice jar. Can be made with beyond Steak tips or seitan. Serves 4. Note: Authentic lomo saltado calls for aji amarillo paste. Due to it being hard to get at local stores, it is omitted from this recipe.

Ingredients

  • 10 oz - 1 lb of beyond steak tips or seitan
  • 5 Tbsp soy sauce
  • 5 cloves garlic minced
  • 1 Tbsp vegan oyster sauce
  • 1 Tbsp red wine vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon cornstarch
  • 1 red onion, sliced into wedges
  • 3 Roma tomatoes, sliced into wedges
  • 1 scallion, sliced finely
  • Oil for cooking
  • French fries cooked to package directions
  • Cooked rice

Steps

  1. If using Seitan: Add 1 tablespoons soy sauce and roughly half the garlic, to the seitan and allow for the seitan to marinate for 10 minutes. If not using Seitan, use all soy sauce in sauce and all garlic during stir fry.
  2. In a small bowl, combine the remaining 4 tablespoons soy sauce, oyster sauce, red wine vinegar, and cornstarch. Whisk to combine and set aside.
  3. If using beyond steak tips, cook to package directions. If using Seitan cook in lightly oiled pan over medium heat for a few minutes until they start to brown. Set protein aside.
  4. Add oil to hot wok. Add onions and sautee for 1 minute.
  5. Add garlic and tomatoes, sautee for anoter minute.
  6. Add protein into wok and combine.
  7. Add sauce to wok and stir to combine. Cook in wok until sauce thickens, 30 seconds-1 minute.
  8. Add scallions and combine.
  9. Serve with rice and French Fries.
